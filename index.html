Sentli also known as corn is about 10,000 years old. It was known as Sentli or Centli in Nahua speaking communties to which the Mexica or Aztecs as known today belonged.


Sentli was genetically engineered by indigenous peoples of America and its ancestor it's called teosinte.


Did you know?

Sentli is the most harvested grain per year.


Most of Mexican and Central American foods are made mainly with corn such as: chicha, tortilla, tamales, pozol, pinol and more.


In most of Latin America the locals hold a yearly event to celebrate corn and all the foods that are made with corn are presented.
